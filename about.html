About Fresco

Preserving and sharing the vision of a better future

Our Mission

The Fresco Learning Platform is dedicated to preserving and sharing Jacque Fresco's work and vision for a better future. Our platform provides comprehensive access to lectures, courses, and collaborative learning resources that explore innovative approaches to societal design and human development.

Through our community-driven platform, we aim to foster understanding, discussion, and practical implementation of ideas that can lead to positive societal change.
